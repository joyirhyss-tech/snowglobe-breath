Privacy

Effective 27 April 2026

Hush is a sixty-second breath practice. We built it to be used, not to be a vehicle for collecting data about you. This page tells you exactly what happens with information when you open the app.

What we collect on our servers

Nothing. Hush has no account system, no login, no analytics SDK, no advertising, no third-party tracking, and no crash reporter. When you use the app, no information about you, your device, or your session is sent to TPC | AEQ or anyone else.

What stays on your device

Your settings live in your browser or device storage (localStorage). That includes:

  • The mode you last selected (Silver, Gold, or Rainbow)
  • Your display preference (Words, Countdown, or Silent)
  • Whether audio and narration are enabled
  • Your chosen language

These are read by the app each time you open it so your previous choices are remembered. Clearing your browser data, reinstalling the app, or using private/incognito mode resets them.

Feedback you send through Roo

Inside Settings, the About section includes a Tell Roo what's up link. If you tap it and send a message, Roo records what you wrote and which mode you were in. Roo does not record your name, email address, location, or device identifiers. The current build of Hush stores those messages locally on your device only — they do not leave your device until and unless we connect a feedback backend in a future update. If we do, this page will be updated to describe where messages go and who can read them.

HealthKit (when enabled)

If you grant Hush permission to write to Apple Health, the app writes one entry per completed session to the Mindful Minutes category. That entry contains the session length and timestamp. It lives in your Apple Health database. It is not transmitted anywhere by Hush. You can revoke permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health on iOS.

Children

Hush is rated 4+ and contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, no chat, and no user-generated content. It collects no information about anyone. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what Hush collects or how it handles information, we will update the date at the top of this page and describe what changed. Our standing commitment is that any future change will move toward less data, not more.

Contact
